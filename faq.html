Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Taken.

01

What is a blind dating app?

+

A blind dating app is a platform that connects individuals seeking romantic partners without revealing their profile pictures or full identities until a certain point in the interaction. It aims to promote deeper connections based on personalities and interests rather than appearances.

02

How do you match someone on Taken?

+

You can use the Heart feature to like each other to make a match. And see where it takes you two.

03

How do I start a conversation with someone I'm interested in?

+

Once you've matched with someone, you can send them a message via the chat feature. Be polite and respectful in your conversations.

04

Is my information and activity private?

+

We take user privacy seriously so you don't have to worry about anything. Your data is protected, and your activity is private. You can adjust your privacy settings in the app according to your requirements.

05

Is there a minimum age requirement for using the app?

+

Yes, users must be at least 18 years old to use our dating app. Age verification is done before approving the person to create an account on Taken.

06

Is it safe to use this dating app?

+

We prioritize user safety. We have implemented safety features, profile verification options, and guidelines to enhance your security. Please refer to our Safety and Moderation section for more details.

07

How do I report inappropriate behaviour or profiles?

+

You can report any inappropriate behaviour or profiles through the app's reporting feature. Our moderation team will review and take appropriate action towards the user.

08

How can I edit my profile information and photos?

+

You can edit your profile by navigating to your profile settings. From there, you can update your photos, bio, and other details.

09

Can I have access to all the features of Taken for free?

+

While most of the features can be used for free, if you want to up your game and make the most of it, you can always choose from the plans we have to offer.
