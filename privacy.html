How to Delete Your Account

If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, please follow these steps:

  1. Log in to the Taken mobile app.
  2. Navigate to your My Profile page.
  3. Scroll down to the bottom of the page and select the Delete Account button.
  4. Confirm your choice when prompted.

Once deleted, your data will be permanently removed from our systems within 90 days. Should you change your mind, you may reactivate your account by logging in within this 90-day window.

Information We Collect

In order to provide the variety of services available on the Taken App (the "Services"), we may need to collect certain personal information from you. This information generally includes the following:

Active Collection

This refers to information you actively provide when you:

  • Register on the App
  • Browse or interact with features on the App
  • Request demos or other information
  • Make payments via our payment gateway
  • Share details about yourself (such as your interests, preferences, and the type of people you wish to meet)
  • Upload content such as written information, audio, or video clips

Examples of actively collected data include your name, contact details, photos, audio/video recordings, career information, education, and bio.

Passive Collection

This refers to information we collect automatically through technology like tracking codes, cookies, or similar tools to understand how you use the App. This helps us improve your experience and the functionality of the App.

Examples include:

  • The URL of the website you visited before or after the App
  • Your device's browser type and IP address
  • Usage patterns and demographics

Note: We do not actively collect personal information from visitors who do not register or use our Services, but some parts of this Privacy Policy still apply to them.

Details on Actively Collected Information

  • Contact Information: If you reach out to us directly, we may collect your name, email address, phone number, and message content. During registration, we collect your name, email, phone number, profile photos, chats, and any attachments you share.
  • Photo Verification: To prevent fake or duplicate accounts and ensure a safe community, we may request you to upload a photo for verification. We may also collect government-issued ID proofs solely to verify age, which are deleted immediately after verification.
  • Sexual Orientation & Preferences: You may share your gender identity, sexual orientation, and preferences to help us personalize your experience.

  • Surveys & Feedback: Occasionally, we may invite you to participate in surveys, provide testimonials, or join marketing campaigns. Participation is optional and you will be informed at the time of contact.
  • Other Information: We may collect technical information such as your IP address, device MAC ID, browser type, and timestamps during your use of the App.

Details on Passively Collected Information

We use standard server log files that automatically record visitors' information when they access websites. This includes IP addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamps, referring/exit pages, and clicks. This data is not linked to personally identifiable information and is used for analyzing trends, administering the site, tracking user movements, and gathering demographic information.

With your explicit consent, we may collect your geolocation (latitude and longitude) from your device to help show you real users nearby.
